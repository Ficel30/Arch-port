About Me

I specialize in turning architectural dreams into tangible spaces. From drawing board to skyline, my journey is a blend of innovation, grit, and design mastery.

2018: Foundation Phase

Started with basic design, mastering AutoCAD and fundamentals of engineering. Interned on junior architecture builds.

2020: Design & 3D Modeling

Advanced to SketchUp, Blender, and Revit. Worked on interiors, 3D presentations, and real client projects.

2022: Construction & Execution

Led on-site execution and collaborated with engineers. Managed materials and ensured builds met design intent.

2024: Global Recognition

Recognized in architectural expos, featured in design magazines, and expanded into international consultancy.
